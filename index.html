Rik Möhlmann utilizes computer graphics and synthetic sound with a hands-on approach, exploring various aspects of metaphysics and their practical applications / Direct encounters with digital media transform abstract concepts into sounds, images, installations, phone paintings, performances, digital spaces and game environments / Through a meditative lens, a continual process of liberation is practiced within the hypnotic spheres of digital tools and their archetypes / In Möhlmann's work, technology is contemplated upon as an extension of the mind-body complex, becoming a playful vehicle for exploring consciousness and its intertwining with digital media in contemporary life.
