rik mohlmann   &-->     Rik Möhlmann utilizes computer graphics and synthetic sound with a curious and hands-on approach, exploring various aspects of metaphysics and their practical applications like found in classical yoga, esotericism and mysticism. His work is characterized by direct encounters with digital media, transforming abstract concepts into sounds, images, and installations. Recurring in Möhlmann's work are his 'phone paintings' - a collection of quick gestures made on smartphone apps that often evolve into more intricate installations, performances, digital spaces, or game environments. Through a meditative lens, a continual process of liberation is practiced within the hypnotic spheres of digital tools and their archetypes. In Möhlmann's work, technology is contemplated upon as an extension of the mind-body complex, becoming a playful vehicle for exploring consciousness and its intertwining with digital media in contemporary life.
